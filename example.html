A CUP OF MATCHA ?

Matcha or macha is finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea leaves. It is special in two aspects of farming and processing: the green tea plants for matcha are shade-grown for the three to four weeks before harvest, and the stems and veins are removed during processing
